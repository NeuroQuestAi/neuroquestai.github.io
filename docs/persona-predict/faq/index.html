FAQ

Below you will find answers to our most frequently asked questions. If you cannot find the answer you are looking for, please contact us at support@neuroquest.ai or sales@neuroquest.ai

  • Access
  • Billing

​​ Access

​​ How do I get a token to test?

If you are interested in our product, please send us an email to sales@neuroquest.ai, and we will provide you with a token for conducting trial tests. Following the tests, if you wish, we can schedule a video call to learn more about you and your company, and explore how we can assist your business further.

​​ Billing

​​ How much does it cost?

In this service, we charge for the analysis of written content submitted by the customer. Predictive analyses require GPU hardware for processing, while other non-GPU-reliant functions are completely free of charge.

​​ About Service

​​ Is the prediction response in real time?

All our services are built on a serverless architecture. It’s important to note that during the initial analysis, the response time might take slightly longer, up to 25 seconds, as the service ‘warms up.’ After that, the response time typically averages around 7 seconds per prediction when using GPU acceleration. If you require even faster response times, we can create an environment tailored to your specific needs.

​​ Does the service natively support my language?

Our model has been primarily trained in English and Brazilian Portuguese, with an additional multi-language version developed. We prioritize avoiding the use of translation services as they can affect the model’s performance. If you need a model in your specific language, please reach out to us via email at support@neuroquest.ai, and we can discuss customized training options. Our service seamlessly detects the language of the customer’s text and directs predictions to the appropriate model, be it in English, Brazilian Portuguese, or the multi-language version.

​​ Power Skills are included?

We have 3 skills from the Power Skills service included in Persona Predict, at no additional cost, but it is important to note that it is another service, if you want other skills we can add them in a personalized way for you.

​​ Do you create personalized result analysis for my company?

Certainly! We can create customized analyses and reports for your company based on the generated results, including presentations for your team members involved.
